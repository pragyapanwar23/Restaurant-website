About Us

Jane's - A Taste of Home in Dehradun (Established 2022)

Nestled in the charming city of Dehradun, Jane's is a haven for those seeking delicious, home-style food. Jane's brings the warmth and love of a home-cooked meal to your table.

Our menu features a delightful selection of dishes prepared with fresh, seasonal ingredients. We draw inspiration from local flavors and traditional recipes, offering a taste of Dehradun's culinary heritage with a modern twist.
At Jane's, we believe in creating a comfortable and inviting atmosphere for our guests. Our friendly staff is dedicated to providing you with a memorable dining experience. Whether you're a local resident or just visiting Dehradun, Jane's is your perfect spot to relax, unwind, and savor a delicious meal. Come experience the taste of home at Jane's!
